Shepherd's Pie

Shepherd’s Pie is a classic comfort dish with hearty layers of savory ground lamb
(or beef in the cottage pie version) simmered with onions, carrots, peas, and rich gravy.
all tucked beneath a golden, fluffy blanket of mashed potatoes.
Baked until the top is slightly crisp and the filling bubbles.
underneath, it’s warm, filling, and perfect for a cozy meal.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • peas
  • potato
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Cook the beef with the pees and onion
  2. add tomato paste and herbs
  3. boil potato and smash it into paste
  4. mix milk and butter with the smashed potato
  5. on an oven pan paste the coocked beef
  6. add the potato mixture on it
  7. wait on 150-200 celsius degree for 20 min
  8. bonne apetit!
